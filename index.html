From premium finishes to expert support, BRANT provides all professionals need for perfect wood coatings.
Full range
BRANT range covers the needs of paint shops of factories producing furniture, doors, window frames. The range includes polyurethane, acrylic, polyester, nitrocellulose and water-based products for interior and exterior use. Tinting system allows customers to get a coating of any color.
Tinting system
Automatic tinting system is based on in-house pigment pastes suitable for wood and metal coatings. Regularly updated formula database is available online. The Colorfactory team supports distributors with the design of tinting facilities and all necessary equipment (dosing machines, shakers, spectrophotometers).
Technical support
Highly experienced Colorfactory tech team provides technical support to BRANT dealers and consumers. They organize product testing at factories, implementation of technologies, adjustment of application lines, produce trend catalogs and samples.
